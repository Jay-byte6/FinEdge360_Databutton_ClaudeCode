## SEBI Regulations Analysis

### When SEBI Registration IS Required

According to [SEBI (Investment Advisers) Regulations, 2013](https://www.sebi.gov.in/sebi_data/attachdocs/1369826090264.pdf):

An entity needs SEBI registration if it:
1. **Provides investment advice** for a fee
2. **Recommends specific securities** (stocks, bonds, mutual funds)
3. **Offers personalized investment strategy** tailored to individual clients
4. **Manages or influences** investment decisions
5. **Charges advisory fees** (flat fee, percentage of AUA, etc.)

**Sources:**
- [SEBI Investment Advisor Registration Requirements](https://www.sebi.gov.in/sebi_data/attachdocs/1369826090264.pdf)
- [SEBI Registered Investment Advisor: Meaning & Eligibility](https://www.equentis.com/blog/sebi-registered-investment-advisor-meaning-eligibility/)
- [How to Become SEBI Registered Investment Advisor](https://taxationconsultancy.in/investmentadvisor-process)

### When SEBI Registration NOT Required ‚úÖ

SEBI registration is NOT required for:
1. ‚úÖ **Educational tools** and calculators
2. ‚úÖ **Generic information** about financial planning
3. ‚úÖ **Self-service platforms** where users make own decisions
4. ‚úÖ **Mathematical calculators** without personalized advice
5. ‚úÖ **Free informational content**

**FinEdge360 falls into this category.**
